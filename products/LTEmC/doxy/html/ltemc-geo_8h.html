enum geoMode_tag

Enum indicating geo-fence boundary events of interest.

Enumerator
geoMode_noUrc 

Do not generate an URC (event) for geo-fence boundary crossing.

geoMode_enterUrc 

Generate an URC (event) for the device entering the geo-fenced area.

geoMode_exitUrc 

Generate an URC (event) for the device leaving the geo-fenced area.

geoMode_bothUrc 

Generate an URC (event) for the device entering or exiting the geo-fenced area.

◆ geoShape_tag

enum geoShape_tag

Enum indicating the shape of the geo-fence boundary.

Enumerator
geoShape_circlerad 

The geo-fence is a circle and is described with a center point and a radius.

geoShape_circlept 

The geo-fence is a circle and is described with a center point and a point on the perimeter.

geoShape_triangle 

The geo-fence is a triangle and is described with the points of the 3 corners.

geoShape_quadrangle 

The geo-fence is a quadrangle and is described with the points of the 4 corners.
