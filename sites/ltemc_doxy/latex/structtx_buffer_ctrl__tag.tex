\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Struct for a I\+OP transmit (TX) buffer control block. Tracks progress of chunk sends to L\+T\+Em1. 

L\+T\+Em1 S\+PI bridge works with chunks of $\sim$64 bytes (actual transfers are usually 58 -\/ 62 bytes). I\+OP abstracts S\+PI chunks from senders.
