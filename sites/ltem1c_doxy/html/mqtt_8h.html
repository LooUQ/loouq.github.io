enum mqttQos_tag

Enum specifying the MQTT QOS for a publish and subscribed topics.

Enumerator
mqttQos_0 

QOS 0: At most once (aka send and forget)

mqttQos_1 

QOS 1: At least once.

mqttQos_2 

QOS 2: Exactly once.

mqttQos_maxAzure 

MS Azure IoTHub max QOS (setting to QOS2 will force disconnect)
